About Me

Hi! I'm Yvanne Jhube Cortes from Cebu, Philippines. I'm passionate about food, cooking, and sharing delicious recipes with everyone. This blog is my little space to share my love for food, cooking tips, and food reviews. Let's enjoy the flavors together!

Some of my favorite dishes include Adobo, Sinigang, and Lechon—and of course, a variety of desserts!

My Latest Recipes

Classic Filipino Adobo

Adobo is a famous Filipino dish made with meat, vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices. It's a flavor-packed comfort food!

Lechon: The King of Filipino Parties

Lechon is a whole roasted pig that's cooked over an open flame, creating a crispy skin and tender meat that's packed with flavor.
